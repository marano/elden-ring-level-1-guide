# The Level 1 Lands Between — Elden Ring RL1 Cheese Route

A power-first, **Rune Level 1** (no level-ups, Wretched) playthrough guide for **Elden Ring** and **Shadow of the Erdtree** — solo/offline, legit cheese only, **no glitches**.

**▶ Live guide: https://marano.github.io/elden-ring-level-1-guide/**

## What's inside

- **The Doctrine** — why status effects, weapon upgrades, and spirit ashes beat a frozen stat sheet.
- **Loadout & Cheese Toolkit** — the weapons/talismans that carry the run, plus reusable terrain/AI cheeses (Margit's Shackle, cliff-drops, the Mimic-Tear strip, etc.).
- **Power Priority** — a ranked ladder of level-independent multipliers and a "be this strong by each boss" table.
- **Interactive Playthrough Checklist** — one cross-region to-do list of ~99 steps, ordered to front-load early power. Progress is saved in your browser, and every location links to its Fextralife page.
- **Boss Cheat-Sheet** — the one-line answer for every wall.

Single self-contained `index.html` — no build step, no dependencies. Light/dark theme aware.
